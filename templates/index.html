Stock Advisor

📋 Available Commands:

  • /newadvice "TICKER" - Get new investment advice for a stock
  • /updateadvice "TICKER" - Update existing advice for a stock
  • /recall "TICKER" - Retrieve previously stored advice
  • /cache "TICKER" - View cached price/PE/PB/ROE snapshot
  • /stats or /stats "TICKER" - View audit statistics
  • /audits or /audits "TICKER" - View recent audit records
